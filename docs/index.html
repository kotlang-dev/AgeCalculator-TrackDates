Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2025

Age Calculator – Track Dates ("the App"), developed by Mohammad Arif, is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our practices concerning data.

1. Data Collection & Usage

The App is designed to function entirely offline. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data from our users. All information you enter, such as dates and occasion names, is stored exclusively in a local database on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, including analytics, advertising networks, or cloud storage providers. Your data is never shared with any external entity.

3. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, it is permanently deleted from your device if you choose to uninstall the App.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from users, including children under the age of 13. The App's functionality is self-contained on the user's device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
